About Us

Araujo Concrete Floor Inc. specializes in concrete floors - dry pack (cement tile) and tile. In 2000, HW Araujo was established and in July 2011, we became incorporated as Araujo Concrete Floor Inc. After 20 years experience, mostly in Ontario, we have grown and decided to expand into preparing subfloor, and professional tile installation. We feel that it is most beneficial to the customer having one company providing complete services from start to finish.
